# 📊 Pandemic Insights and Impact Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic had a massive global impact, affecting millions of lives and economies worldwide. Understanding trends in confirmed cases, deaths, and recoveries is crucial for analyzing the severity and progression of the pandemic.

In this project, I developed an interactive dashboard to analyze pandemic data and generate meaningful insights across countries and regions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main objectives of this project were:

* To track **total confirmed, death, and recovered cases**
* To analyze **monthly trends of the pandemic**
* To compare **country-wise performance**
* To evaluate **recovery rate and case fatality rate (CFR)**

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard:

* Cleaned missing and inconsistent values
* Standardized country and region names
* Ensured numeric fields were correctly formatted
* Removed duplicates and aggregation errors

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a **comprehensive view of pandemic statistics** with interactive filtering.

---

### 🔹 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* Total Confirmed Cases: **829M**
* Total Deaths: **43M**
* Total Recovered Cases: **388M**
* Active Cases: **397M**
* Recovery Rate: **46.88%**
* Case Fatality Rate (CFR): **5.24%**

---

### 📈 Monthly Trend Analysis

A line chart visualizes:

* Growth in confirmed cases
* Increase in recoveries
* Death trends over time

👉 This helps understand how the pandemic evolved month by month.

---

### 🌍 Country-wise Analysis

* Top countries like **US, Brazil, Russia, and India** contribute significantly
* Bar charts highlight comparisons between confirmed, deaths, and recoveries

---

### 🥧 Distribution Analysis

* **Top 5 countries by CFR**
* **Recovery rate distribution by region**

👉 These charts provide quick insights into severity and recovery efficiency.

---

### 🗺️ Geographic Visualization

A world map shows the spread of cases globally, helping identify heavily affected regions.

---

## 🎛️ Interactivity

The dashboard includes filters for:

* Country
* WHO Region
* Date

👉 Users can dynamically explore data and generate customized insights.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* The US reported the highest number of confirmed cases
* Recovery trends improved over time
* Certain regions showed higher fatality rates
* Active cases remained high during peak periods
